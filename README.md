Clasicul joc de cărți șeptică implementat in C.


                    HOW TO PLAY:
THE GOAL:
Collect points! There are 8 total points in the deck:
All four 10s (1 pt each) and all four Aces (1 pt each).

THE RULES:
1. The leader plays a card. The rank of this card becomes
   the 'target' for the trick.
2. The other players must play a card.
3. If someone plays a card matching the target rank, OR
   plays a 7, they 'cut' the trick and are currently winning.
4. If the trick is cut, the leader gets a chance to 'reply'
   by playing another matching card or a 7. If they do, the
   trick continues and the opponents MUST play another card.
5. Whoever last cut the trick wins all the points inside it!
6. Players draw back up to 4 cards, and the winner leads next.
